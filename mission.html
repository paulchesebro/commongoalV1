Our Mission

We support young refugees in our community who have a passion to play soccer. By working together, we create inclusive, competitive, team experiences that foster cross-cultural friendships and provide pathways for healthy social, emotional, and physical development.


We all win.


The world's most loved sport has the power to help our new neighbors find community, build responsibility, and gain confidence. Yet they face many barriers and are often left out.


With a focus on middle school and high school age soccer enthusiasts, we provide logistical and financial support needed in order for them to participate in soccer programs, including club and school teams, camps, and tournaments. We partner with local groups and individuals to bring these opportunities to life.


The broader Missoula community is enriched by the participation of new arrivals, through our differences and what we share in common. Through soccer, we are building connections, reciprocal friendships, and broadening understanding and appreciation of the world outside of Montana.
