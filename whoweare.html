Our Purpose

We are a Missoula-based nonprofit working to build a more inclusive community. We believe in the power of finding joy and connection through group activity.We have seen firsthand how team experiences, on and off the field, foster friendships, promote healthy lifestyles, offer stability, and help youth increase their self-responsibility, positive outlook, and hope for the future.
